— Our Story —

A boutique born from a love letter to flowers.

梨花's Flower Shop began as a small atelier with a single idea: that every flower, when chosen with care, can carry a feeling words cannot. Today, our boutique next to Kun Shan University is a quiet sanctuary of blush peonies, soft roses, and gilded ribbons.

We believe flowers are more than décor — they are the language of love, celebration, and remembrance. Each arrangement is composed by hand, with the patience of a couturier and the eye of an artist.

  • Elegant · Feminine · Romantic
  • Warm · Modern · Vibrant
  • Freshness, quality, and a touch of gold.
